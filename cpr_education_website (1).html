About Us

Heartbeat CPR Initiative is a youth-led organization founded by Leena Manadan. We are dedicated to promoting hands-only CPR training for all, especially in underserved and vulnerable populations.

Why CPR Matters:

  • Over 350,000 out-of-hospital cardiac arrests occur in the U.S. annually.
  • Nearly 90% of people who suffer an out-of-hospital cardiac arrest die.
  • Immediate CPR can double or triple a cardiac arrest victim’s chance of survival.
  • 70% of cardiac arrests happen at home — your loved one could be the one who needs help.

Our Mission

We strive to equip individuals, especially those in high-risk or underserved communities, with hands-only CPR training. No one is too old or too young to learn how to save a life. Through workshops, demonstrations, and school partnerships, we work to close the gap in emergency response readiness.
